About Bottle Builder

What is a Bottle Building?

In a bottle building, the walls are made by filling plastic bottles with sand, dirt or some other fill. These "bottle bricks" are then laid down in rows, with a layer of cement in between each row and column of bottles.

Not only are bottle buildings a great use of plastic bottles, they are very durable. I cannot confirm this, but according to various sources, these structures are: bulletproof, earthquake resistant, fireproof, and temperature retaining.

To find out more about bottle buildings, check out these search results.

What is Bottle Builder?

Bottle Builder is a tool to provide a theoretical estimate of the resources that one would need to create a bottle building. The user provides the resource information (bottle type/dimensions, fill information, foundation depth, building information, etc.) and Bottle Builder estimates the number of bottles, the amount of fill and the amount of cement needed for this building.

Bottle Builder is NOT: a tool to calculate structural integrity, a tool for determining the building codes in a user's location, nor is it a tool to give a 100% accurate calculation of necessary resources.

I already have a house, what use is this to me?

You don't need to build a whole house to take advantage of this cool sustainable building technique!

Some possible structures you can build: doggie homes, greenhouses, sheds.
